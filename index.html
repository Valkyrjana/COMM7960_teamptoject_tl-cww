Why we here

It is important for people to understand the benefits of AI for society because AI has the potential to revolutionize many aspects of our lives, from healthcare and education to transportation. By understanding these benefits, people can make informed decisions about to best implement and utilize AI in ways that will improve the world around us. Additionally, understanding the benefits of AI can help to dispel fears and misconceptions about the technology, leading to more productive and positive discussions about its future role in society and discovering more advantages in individual life.


What is AI？

AI is not a silver bullet, but it could help tackle some of the world's most challenging social problems. Artificial intelligence (AI) is a wide-ranging branch of computer science concerned with building smart machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. While AI is an interdisciplinary science with multiple approaches, advancements in machine learning and deep learning, in particular, are creating a paradigm shift in virtually every sector of the tech industry.


Where is the level of development of AI?

Artificial intelligence (AI) has made significant progress over the years, and is now at an unprecedented level of development. In recent years, there have been numerous breakthroughs in AI research, which have led to the creation of incredibly advanced systems that can perform tasks that were once considered impossible for machines.


Deep learning algorithms

One major advancement in AI has been the development of deep learning algorithms, which are modeled after the structure and function of the human brain. These algorithms allow machines to learn from large amounts of data and make accurate predictions, classifications, and decisions based on that data. This has resulted in significant improvements in areas such as speech recognition, image recognition, natural language processing, and self-driving cars.


Robotics

Another important development in AI is in the field of robotics, where AI-powered robots are being used to perform a wide range of tasks that were previously performed by humans. These robots are highly intelligent and capable of interacting with their environment in a way that was not possible before. They are also able to work collaboratively with humans, making them ideal for use in industries such as manufacturing and healthcare.



Machine Learning

In addition to these advancements, there have also been significant developments in the field of machine learning, which is a subset of AI that focuses on developing algorithms that can learn and improve from data without being explicitly programmed. This has led to the creation of powerful models that can make accurate predictions in various domains, including finance, healthcare, and marketing.
